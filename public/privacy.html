Overview
Bara ("we," "our," or "the app") is a step-tracking challenge app for iOS. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We believe in collecting only what's necessary to make the app work.
Health Data
Bara reads your daily step count from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. We request read-only access — we do not write to or modify your Health data in any way. Your step count is synced to our server solely to display your daily progress and power head-to-head weekly challenges.
Your HealthKit data is never used for advertising, is never sold to third parties, and is never shared with third parties for marketing or data-brokering purposes.
You can revoke Health access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Bara. If you revoke access, the app will no longer read your step count and background sync will stop.
Information We Collect
Apple ID identifier
Account creation and sign-in via Sign in with Apple. We receive only the opaque user identifier Apple provides — we do not access your Apple ID password.
Display name
A name you choose so friends can find and challenge you. Visible to other Bara users.
Daily step count
Read from HealthKit to show progress and calculate challenge results. Stored on our server with a date stamp.
Step goal
An optional daily goal you set to track personal progress.
Friends list & requests
To enable friend discovery, challenge matchups, and showing friends' step progress.
Challenge & stake data
Records of your weekly challenges, matchups, and negotiated stakes with friends.
APNs device token
Registered with our server to deliver push notifications about challenge updates. Stored only while notifications are enabled.
Sign in with Apple
Bara uses Sign in with Apple as its only authentication method. When you sign in, Apple provides us with an opaque user identifier and, if you choose to share it, an email address. If you choose "Hide My Email," we receive only Apple's relay address — we never see your real email. We do not access your Apple ID password.
How We Use Your Data
All data we collect is used exclusively to operate the app: to show your daily step count and goal progress, to run weekly challenges and display head-to-head results, to let friends find and challenge you, and to send you notifications about challenge activity when you've opted in. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.
Data Sharing
Your step count and display name are visible to friends you've accepted in the app. This is necessary for the challenge feature — if you're in a head-to-head challenge, your opponent can see your step progress for that week. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for their own use. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs or advertising frameworks.
Storage & Security
Your data is stored on our server infrastructure. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Session tokens are used for authentication and are refreshed regularly. Locally on your device, the app caches session credentials, health-authorization state, notification preferences, and your display name and step goal using iOS local storage.
Data Deletion
You can sign out at any time from the Settings tab, which clears your local session data and unregisters your device from push notifications. To request complete deletion of your account and all data from our servers, email support@steptracker-api.org. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
Children's Privacy
Bara is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
Changes & Contact
If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page with a new effective date. Continued use of Bara after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Questions or concerns? Reach us at support@steptracker-api.org.
